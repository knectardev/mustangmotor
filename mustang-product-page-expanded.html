The front seat sets the rider back 1.75" compared to the stock seat plus it angles the body for long-distance comfort. The rear seat provides support across its full width and keeps the passenger firmly in the comfort zone. Styled after state-of-the-art automotive interior designs, the seat features exquisite stitching detail. Available driver backrest sold separately. The receiver for the optional, removable and fully adjustable driver backrest is built directly into the seat's baseplate.

Will Fit the 2024 Road King Special, Road Glide Limited, Ultra Limited, Tri Glide Ultra
*(Does not fit 2023-2024 CVO Models)

Note: This seat will not work with Mustang or H-D® frame-mounted backrest kits. Seat will fit with handrail.

Installation of the heated seats on 2014 and later FL Touring models require use of Harley adaptor:
69200722 Fits '14-'16 Touring and Trike models.
69201599 Fits '18-'24 Softail models and '17-later Touring (except '23-later FLHXSE, FLTRXSE, '24-later FLHX, FLTRX, FLTRXSTSE and '25-later FLHXU) and Trike as a mid-bike connector (near the seat).
